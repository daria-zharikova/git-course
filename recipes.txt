Baked, fried, or mashed, there's no wrong way to cook potatoes. The epitome of comfort food, there's nothing we love more than cozying up to potatoes in any (and every) form. Whether they're the center of your meal, a bite-sized app (hello, potato skin bites!), or a filling, easy side to your main course, potatoes can truly do it all. While we included all the basics—roasted, hash browns, mashed taters, fries—we also threw in some creative takes on the famous spud. So if you need some weeknight inspo, are looking for a late-night snack, or need a crowd-pleasing potato side dish for your next party, try whipping up one of these flavor-packed recipes to switch things up in the most delicious way.
